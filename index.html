Burning It Up!

Hailing from the granite state of New Hampshire, I am currently enrolled at Michigan State University majoring in Games and Interactive media. Outside of academics you can find me reading comics, designing games or running Niche! A student run game show at MSU. I’ve spent my past couple summers at YMCA camp Mi-Te-Na where I’ve been able to work on physical playtests of some of my personal projects. In my spare time I enjoy outdoor activities like snowboarding and camping. Growing up in New Hampshire I learned how to handle the cold, that’s why I always try to bring the HEAT!
