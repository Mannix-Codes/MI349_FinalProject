About Me

Hi, I'm Mannix! I'm a game and web designer with a passion for creating narrative driven interactive epics.

My game design journey began at summercamp where I created engaging physical games for campers during the day, and brought cabins through detailed choose your own adventure stories at night. As a student at Michigan State University, I have honed my skills in game design and production, working on various projects in both the Games and Interactive Media Major, and the Game Design and Development, and Computer Science minors.
