Figma Design

About

This project began as an assignment for my web design class on creating a basic figma prototype. After beginning though I decided to continue working on the project to further develop my skills and understanding of figma, and other design principles.

  • Programs Used
  • -Figma

Design

The original assignment tasked us with developing a basic shopping page for a small (fictional) business. I focused on creating a clean and intuitive user interface, ensuring that the navigation and product display were user-friendly and visually appealing.

Prototyping

After fulfilling the requirements of the assignment, I continued to explore Figma's prototyping features. I created interactive elements, linked pages, and tested user flows to ensure a smooth and intuitive experience. I also worked with figma variables to create dynamic content that could change based on user interactions throughout the design

Where I Succeeded

This project helped me learn a lot more about figma, prototyping, and user interface design. I gained valuable experience in creating interactive prototypes and understanding user flows, which will be beneficial for future design projects.

Where I Will Learn More

I plan to continue exploring Figma's advanced features, including more complex prototyping, component libraries, and collaborative design workflows. This will help me create more sophisticated and user-friendly designs in future projects.
